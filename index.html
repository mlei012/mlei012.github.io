MING LEI

Ming Lei

I am currently working as a postdoctoral fellow at Vanderbilt University. My primary interest is utilizing various computational techniques to explore and characterize materials related to energy. During my five years of Ph.D. study, I extensively focused on computational theory research, specifically in the areas of computational materials design, interface catalysis, and the optical and magnetic properties of materials. Additionally, I have gained research experience in the field of electrochemistry and energy storage systems, particularly in the areas of lithium-ion batteries, lithium-sulfur batteries, and lithium-oxygen batteries.
